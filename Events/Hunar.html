HUNAR

- Talent Show -

( 26 August 2018 to 15 September 2018 )

About

Real talent shines through regardless of how many others there are around you.

The CREATIVE BEAST organization planned a Talent Showcase event- HUNAR, where everyone has a chance to prove their talent without being bound to any limits as we all know that a person's skill has no boundaries to improve with Hardwork and Determination towards it.

HUNAR is a national level event where we gave a floor for everyone to showcase their skills and grow with it on a competitive stage while winning prizes. Hunar Event gives members a step to highlight their singing & rapping, dancing, modeling, poetry, song &story writing, skit, beatboxing, drama, and many more different talents on a national level. The Finalist of ROADIES 10 ‘RAMANDEEP KAUR’ and Famous Indian Designer ‘SIDDHARTH KAKKAR’ comes as a special guest. In this event, the Vice president of the “Dance Sport Council Of India” gave his speech on occasion to build the confidence of participants and be the judge for the competition.

With the slogan, let's helps people develop and improve ourselves meanwhile. The Creative Beast had tried to give everyone a platform to showcase their skills and make their day full of unforgetable memories to remember till all life. A small step to bring their hidden talent to a stage can give them a meaningful purpose to grow and believe in themselves.
