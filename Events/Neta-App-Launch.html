NETA APP LAUNCHING

( 22 August 2018 )

About

“Neta App” Launch was one of the biggest event that was held in President house at Delhi. The Neta App was launched by Sir Pratham Mittal. The motive behind this Neta App was to help citizens with the platform where they can give reviews to various politician in their region and rate them on the basis of their work done in particular city or state.

This app is playing an important role for grading the politicians and know about them. Additionally, it also tracks that citizen giving review is authentic or not. In this way it provides us with unbiased results.

The launching of Neta App was done in the presence of former president Shri Pranab Mukherjee. Various other delegates also joined this app launching. Chief minister of Delhi Sir Arvind Kejriwal, former chairman of BCCI, Sir Anurag Thakur and many more delegates were present. This great event was managed by different organizations at LPU and student organization Creative Beast was one of those organizations selected among 200 organizations to be the part of this event management team at President house. We managed whole event starting from welcoming the guests to the final wrap of the event. All the necessary resources, planning of event, decoration, food was managed and planned by us.

We were pleased to work in this grand event of app launching and had a good experience there.
