About

We hear music from the moment we are born and it continues throughout our lives because its an important part of our life. Music is the way of expressing yourself and we can connect our day to day life with music in some or the other form. Rapping is another way of expressing your thoughts and action. Both rap and music is connected to each other. Rap makes peoples thought worth and help them to describe their pain, love, anger and various other feeling.

Akash Dadlani who is one of the famous Rapper of India gained his popularity among people with his singing skills and his fans call him A-Cash. Akash Dadlani is also popularly known for his participation in reality show Big Boss 11. He was one of the most entertaining contestants of the Big Boss house. He worked with various famous singer like Meet Brothers and Sukhwinder Singh and many more. He had shoot one of his famous song “Bang Bang” at Lovely Professional University. Akash Dadlani and DJ KVTA came for his song Promotion at LPU and student organization Creative Beast organized the launching event of his song at LPU. All the management and Planning of the event was done by Creative Beast.

Creative Beast made this promotional event a huge success where more than 1.5K students witnessed this event. Students had lot of fun with Akash Dadlani and DJ KVTA, also he shared his journey of becoming a successful rapper. Everyone enjoyed their Performances.
