About

One should never lose an opportunity of seeing anything beautiful because beauty itself is God’s handwriting.

BHARAT SUNDARI is a National Level beauty pageant in India organized by Student Organization CREATIVE BEAST while discovering our “Crowning Beauty of Nation.” This event has two Categories for only Miss Bharat sundari at a national competition stage for winner crown of Bharat Sundari at lovely professional university.

‘To be beautiful means to be yourself. You don’t need to be accepted by others, you need to accept yourself.’

The creative beast organization also believes in beauty from the heart and soul of a person. So, when the next time you think of beautiful things, don’t forget to count yourself in.
