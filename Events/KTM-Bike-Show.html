KTM BIKE SHOW

- Stunt Show -

( October 2019 )

About

A biker said a unique quote about his love regarding bikes that," Sometimes, you found yourself in the middle of nowhere, you find your true self." The passion in the soul motives to race with the wind on Bike while moving on the road.

An exciting day full of breathtaking stunts on bikes between bike lovers at Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar.

Student Organization CREATIVE BEAST organized a KTM bike stunt show to entertain the passionate bike lovers successfully. As mesmerizing bike stunts left the vast crowd go crazy and astonished having watched the stunning moves of the bikers live. Every remarkable bike stunt, coupled with the hooting of the amazed students was an icing to the cake for the event.

CREATIVE BEAST feels delighted hosting events for students, staff's entertainment in the middle of rough college life. It s always been our motto to bring a smile on your face.
