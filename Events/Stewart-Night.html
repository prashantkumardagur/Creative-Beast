STEWART NIGHT

- EDM Night -

( 8 August 2019 )

About

"Let's make the opening day of college life more wonderful with some new friends to remember the rest of your life." A College fresher's party always brings a mesmerizing time of pleasure and unforgettable memories with welcoming new friends in our life.

The CREATIVE BEAST organization planned a Freshers party STEWARD NIGHT. To welcome new students to the college, a party is the best choice where everyone can enjoy a night with new people having passion, spirits while being free of stress in a far from the home conditions.

Steward night was a fresher welcoming celebration for our new students of Boys Hostel-5 & 6 at Lovely Professional University. This party lets new students to highlight their singing & rapping, dancing, modeling, and other talents among their new student groups and become families quickly with new people and surroundings of the place.

Creative Beast always find satisfaction in everyone's happiness, more if we are the reason behind it. The Creative Beast had tried to give students a stage to showcase their skills and make their day full of unforgetable memories between friends to remember till all life. A small step to bring a smile on their opening day of college can give them significant confidence to grow with the upcoming days of college life.
