ABOUT

Creative Beast Studio is a Punjab based Studio that provides a digital platform to the best talent among us to endeavour their talent worldwide. The Creative Beast Studio was founded by Prem Kumar and Sumeet Kumar on 3rd February 2020. It is a Talent Show in which participants perform the arts of singing, dancing, poetry telling, storytelling, acting, playing instrument, comedy and other activities to showcase their skills. This Creative Beast Studio works on rising hidden talent and furnishes them with one single platform to carry on with their dreams. This Platform encourages genius minds to follow their dream and express their creativity.
