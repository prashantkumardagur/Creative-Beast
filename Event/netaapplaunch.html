The motive behind this Neta App was to help citizens with the platform where they can give reviews to various politician in their region and rate them on the basis of their work done in particular city or state.

This app is playing an important role for grading the politicians and know about them. Additionally, it also tracks that citizen giving review is authentic or not. In this way it provides us with unbiased results.

This great event was managed by different organizations at LPU and student organization Creative Beast was one of those organizations selected among 200 organizations to be the part of this event management team at President house.
