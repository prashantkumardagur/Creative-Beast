Location

About

Indian Science Congress is the national conference that is held every year in India. The five-day-long event aims to bring together science fraternity across the world to discuss scientific innovation and research. It is always held in the month of January. The 106th Indian Science Congress was graced by the presence of Honourable Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi. LPU becomes the first private university in India to host the Honourable PM. Cabinet Ministers including HRD, Law, IT, Science and Technology visited LPU. Nobel Laureates and scientists from all over the world helped the millennials in imbibing the scientific fervour for shaping the emerging research in Science & Technology.

The focal theme of this event was "FUTURE INDIA: SCIENCE&TECHNOLOGY". It was one of the biggest conferences held at LPU. In this conference Student Organization, Creative Beast was also the part in managing this biggest conference of India.

All the decoration part was managed by the Creative Beast Organization. Additionally, food department management was done by us. We contributed our part to the biggest conference and gave our 100% contribution in making this National level Conference a successful event.

The Science Exhibition was one of the major events of the 106th Indian Science Congress. It served as a platform towards inspiring the students, academicians and the common man, to appreciate the value of science and to cultivate scientific attitude. Major exhibitors include CSIR, ISRO, DAE, ICMR, DST, DRDO, ICAR and more.

We gained very good experience in managing this intellectual Conference and we are happy to be the part of this International Event.
